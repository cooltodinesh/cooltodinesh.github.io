Tutorial 3 - Promoting an advertisment

Step 1. Activate an Advertisement

Browse through the ads allocated to you. Choose the one you like. If app shows no ads allocated to you, work on increasing you social connect. Watch the space, till ads get allocated to you.

Once you like the advertisement, click on Activate if you would decide to promote it to your network.

Step 2. Share on Whatsapp

You can share the advertisement to your whatsapp contacts both by sharing it to your status and also by sending it directly to inidividual or group chats.

Step 3. Share on Instagram

You can promote the advertisement by sharing it on instgram by sharing it on instagram and FB stories as well as on individual chats.

While sharing on Instagram, copy the advertisement link from AdCast app and paste the same along with your story.

Step 4. Share on Facebook

You can promote the advertisement on Facebook by adding it to your story or by posting it on your wall.

While sharing on Facebook, copy the advertisement link from AdCast app and paste the same along with your post.

Step 5. Share manually on other digital media like blogs and other social apps.

You can promote the advertisement on any other digital media of your own by downloading the image assets and copying the advertisement link from Adcast app.
